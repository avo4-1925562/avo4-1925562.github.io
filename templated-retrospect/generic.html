About Us

A little bit about our small business.

Our little company of five hundred factories around the world started in 2018, when Mark Zuckerberg testified in front of congress.

Like everyone else, we noticed his infamous cushion booster seat and thought - what if every short person could be tall enough for their desk?

So we did a little research, talked to some investors, and BAM the Zuckercushion was born.
